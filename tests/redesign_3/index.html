GU League was officially launched on 23 of January 2005 and we can proudly say that more than 20,000 official matches have been played since!

New to GU League?

  1. Register your callsign on the official forum (click on register tab, top right side): The BZFlag forum is here.
  2. Now that your tank is registered, you have to be able to spawn(play). To do that, login on this site using your registered callsign and password and message one of the administrative team (contact page) to get your callsign on GU league group list. If you are not on the list your callsign cannot spawn or talk.
  3. Last but not least, you can have one callsign on the GU league group list only, and you can belong to one team only. ONE person, ONE callsign, ONE team. Simple. Double identities on the GU league group list or belonging to more than one team will get you banned. We are very serious about this as it is considered as cheating.
